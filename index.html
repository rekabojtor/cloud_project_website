Puppies for Beginners

Quick basics for first-time puppy owners: food, training, socialization, and care.

What puppies need

Puppies are curious, bitey, and energetic. A simple routine + gentle training goes a long way.

Daily basics

  • Short training sessions (5–10 min), lots of praise.
  • Potty breaks after sleep, play, and meals.
  • Safe chewing toys to protect your hands and furniture.

Health + care

  • Vet plan: vaccines, deworming, flea/tick as needed.
  • Gradual socialization (new people, sounds, places).
  • Grooming depends on coat—start handling paws/ears early.
